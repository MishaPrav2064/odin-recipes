Tomato and Mozzarella Salad

Description

Tomato and Mozzarella SaladWhen it comes to a light and fresh salad, this Tomato & Mozzarella Salad is perfect! This colorful salad can be paired with anything off the grill or just eat it by itself as a meal.

This Tomato & Mozzarella Salad takes less that 15 minutes to make and is completely portable if taking to a picnic or summer bbq. And with only five ingredients, itâ€™s likely you already have what you need to put it together.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices alternately on a plate.
  2. Place fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer.
