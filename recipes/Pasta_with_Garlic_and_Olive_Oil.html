Pasta with Garlic and Olive Oil

Description

Pasta with Garlic and Olive Oil This humble and fragrant spaghetti with garlic and olive oil brings me back to my childhood. It’s a classic Italian pasta dish that requires a few cheap ingredients, little effort, and it’s ready in 10 minutes.

It’s a super quick spaghetti recipe, delicious and packed with flavor, which comes in handy any time. Absolutely perfect for sharing with friends or after a long day, when you decide pasta is for dinner tonight.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic slices and sauté until golden.
  4. Stir in chili flakes, salt, and pepper.
  5. Toss in the cooked spaghetti and mix well.
  6. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve.
